Frequently Asked Questions

Is the acoustic chime jarring? +
No. We engineered the acoustics to mimic a soft singing bowl or a calming spa notification. It alerts gently without startling.
What happens if Wi-Fi disconnects? +
LinkCare relies on a redundant 5G cellular failover system. If the local network drops, the device autonomously secures a connection, ensuring the caregiver is always updated.
How do I clean the medical-grade compartments? +
The pill arrays are fully removable and dishwasher safe on a gentle, low-heat cycle.
